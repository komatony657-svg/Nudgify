How it works

01

Tell Nudge what you're doing

Type or say what you're working on and why it matters to you. Takes five seconds.

02

It checks in on you

At your chosen interval, Nudgify quietly asks if you're still on track. No alarms. Just a gentle tap on the shoulder.

03

If you drift, it finds you

No response? One gentle follow-up. Still nothing? It backs off and tries again later. No guilt, no spam.

04

Coming back is easy

When you return, Nudge shows you what you were doing and the reason you said it mattered — so re-entry doesn't feel like starting over.
